01 Hours compliance — 724h rolling 3-month limit

Flagged workers: any consecutive 3-month window ≥ 652h (close) or ≥ 724h (breach). Highlighted cells = worst window.

Checks every possible consecutive 3-month window within the reporting period against the 724 h limit. Only workers whose worst window reaches the warning threshold (652 h, 90 % of limit) are listed. The highlighted month cells show which 3-month window is the worst for each worker; the remaining months are dimmed. Workers already in breach are shown in red with the overage amount; those approaching the limit are shown in amber. Hours are calculated after removing stand-down days (any 14th consecutive working day in an unbroken roster run).

—
Limit: 724h per any rolling 3-month period  ·  Close ≥ 652h  ·  Breach ≥ 724h  ·  Stand-down applied: day 14+ in any consecutive working run is excluded.
