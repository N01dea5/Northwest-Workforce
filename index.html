01 Utilisation — key metrics

Filtered by client & position selections above

Forward committed is the total of all scheduled hours in the next 3 months sourced from confirmed and mobilising roster bookings.

02 Utilisation — Position × Month

3 months behind, current, 3 months forward. Cell tint = MoM retention %.

Each row is a position group; each column is a month. The large number is the headcount of workers with hours that month; the smaller italic is the total hours worked. Cell colour reflects month-on-month retention — the share of last month's workers still active this month (green ≥ 70 %, amber 50–70 %, red < 50 %). Forward months (teal/dashed) show scheduled bookings sourced from the roster; they carry no retention tint. Positions are grouped by discipline.
